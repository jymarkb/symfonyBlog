# Redesign Brief

## Objective

Redesign the product as a modern editorial platform with a clearer public narrative and a cleaner authoring experience than the current Symfony UI.

## Public-Site Goals

- stronger homepage story and information hierarchy
- clearer featured/latest/most-viewed content presentation
- more polished blog index and article reading experience
- SEO-ready public routes from day one

## Dashboard Goals

- simpler post creation and editing flow
- more intentional content status and publishing states
- better media handling and preview feedback
- consistent tables, forms, dialogs, and notifications

## Design Direction

- distinct visual language rather than a direct reskin
- reusable design tokens and component patterns
- strong typography and layout identity
- mobile and desktop parity

## Non-Goals For The First Build

- full no-code page-builder scope expansion
- migrating every current widget one-to-one
- solving every analytics/reporting feature in v1

## Must Preserve

- support for rich post content
- category-based browsing
- authenticated content management
- room for future analytics and profile features
